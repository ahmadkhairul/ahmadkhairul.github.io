Delightfull Crumbs Web

by Ahmad Khairul Anwar

Project Overview

Delightfull Crumbs Web is a web-based platform that complements the cookie ordering app with informative and engaging articles. In addition to reading various articles about recipes, baking tips, the history of cookies, and product reviews, users can also order cookies directly through the website.

My Contribution

As the UI/UX Designer for the Delightfull Crumbs app, I conducted paper and digital wireframing, as well as low and high-fidelity prototyping.

Sitemap

Paper wireframes

The paper wireframe for the Delightfull Crumbs Website includes a homepage with a main banner, article summaries, and popular categories. The article page displays the title, content, a sidebar with related articles, as well as options for commenting and sharing. The category page allows users to explore articles by category, while the ordering page simplifies the process of selecting and ordering cookies with filters and product details. The footer provides contact information, important links, and a newsletter subscription option. This design is responsive, ensuring an optimal experience on both mobile and desktop devices.

Low-fidelity Prototype

The low-fidelity prototype for the Delightfull Crumbs Website serves as an initial model to illustrate the basic layout and interactions. This prototype showcases the structure of the homepage, including a header with navigation and a main banner. The article page demonstrates how content will be organized with text and image areas. The ordering page displays a list of cookies with filter options and product details. The design is simple, using basic elements like boxes and lines to focus on functionality and user flow without final visual details.
