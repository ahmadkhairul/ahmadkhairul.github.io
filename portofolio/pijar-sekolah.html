Description

In my role as a front-end developer for Pijar Sekolah, I have contributed to various products. Each product is designed to support the management and development of education in Indonesia, both in terms of management systems and user interaction. Here are some of the projects I have been involved in.

  1. Learn-Aja.

    This project aims to develop an interactive online learning platform that is easily accessible to students from various backgrounds. Learn-Aja facilitates adaptive teaching with diverse multimedia content to support effective learning processes.

  2. Learn-Aja.

    This project aims to develop an interactive online learning platform that is easily accessible to students from various backgrounds. Learn-Aja facilitates adaptive teaching with diverse multimedia content to support effective learning processes.

  3. Pijar Kelas (SIM & Student).

    A Student Information Management (SIM) and Student Management system designed to support online class management. Pijar Kelas provides features for class administration, student performance tracking, and integration with evaluation systems.

  4. Pijar Madrasah Find My School.

    A location-based school search feature that helps users, especially students and parents, find madrasas or schools that fit their preferences and location.

  5. Pijar Sekolah CMS Content.

    A Content Management System (CMS) that allows school administrators to manage and update digital content centrally. This CMS includes management of learning materials, announcements, and educational information.

  6. Pijar Sekolah Dashboard Pendidikan.

    This dashboard provides comprehensive reports on academic performance, student participation, and other educational statistics to assist decision-makers in schools or educational institutions.

  7. Pijar Sekolah Dashboard Transaksi.

    A dashboard-based platform for managing and tracking school financial transactions, such as tuition payments, operational funds, and other financial reports, easily and transparently.

  8. Pijar Sekolah Digital Book View.

    This feature allows students and teachers to access textbooks in digital format. With a user-friendly interface, users can read, mark, and highlight book content online.

  9. Pijar Sekolah Exam.

    An online evaluation system designed to help teachers create, manage, and assess student exams digitally, ensuring secure and accurate data.

  10. Pijar Sekolah Exam V2.

    An enhanced version of Pijar Sekolah Exam, featuring improvements such as category-based question management, automated grading, and a refined interface for a better user experience.

  11. Pijar Sekolah FABD.

    This feature provides a solution for managing student attendance and presence data digitally, allowing integration with payment systems and real-time attendance tracking.

  12. Pijar Sekolah School Portal.

    A school portal that serves as the gateway for students, teachers, and parents to access all digital school services, including academic information, class schedules, and other school activities.

  13. Pijar Sekolah V2 (SIM & Student).

    An updated version of the school information management and student management system, offering improved user experience, system performance, and new features to support more efficient school operations.

  14. Pijar Sekolah V3 (SIM & Student).

    The latest version of Pijar Sekolah, introducing a more modern interface and integration with the latest technologies, such as data analytics and machine learning, to provide deeper insights for school management.

  15. Pijar Sekolah View Content.

    A feature that allows students and teachers to view learning content visually and interactively, including videos, learning modules, and other multimedia content, accessible anytime, anywhere.

With my experience in developing various digital education solutions, I am committed to continuously innovating in creating platforms that simplify the teaching and learning process and support more effective education management.
