Project Overview

Landtick is an application that provides train tickets booking services

My Contribution

As the Fullstack Developer for the Landtik app, I handle the full development process, including converting UI designs from Figma into functional code, building the frontend interface, developing the backend infrastructure, and integrating APIs to ensure seamless communication between the client and server. My role involves end-to-end development to create a fully functional and user-friendly application.

As part of the final requirement for graduating from the bootcamp, I worked on the Landtick project. The project allowed me to demonstrate my ability to integrate various technologies, ensure the overall functionality and user experience of the application.
