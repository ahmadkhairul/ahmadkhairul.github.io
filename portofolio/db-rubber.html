Database of Rubber Product in Indonesia 2016

by Ahmad Khairul Anwar

Project Overview

Database Produk Karet 2016, a web-based platform designed to serve as a comprehensive database for the rubber industry in Indonesia. The app tracks the entire supply chain of rubber products, from upstream to downstream, across the country. It provides essential information to stakeholders, facilitating better decision-making and improving the efficiency of the rubber industry. This project highlights my ability to create data-driven web applications that support industry-specific needs on a national scale.

My Contribution

As the Software Engineer for Database Karet 2016, I oversaw the entire development process, from designing the user interface and user experience to building both the front-end and back-end. Utilizing a comprehensive dataset of rubber products

Project Duration

2016

Backstory

The idea for Database Karet 2016 began when one of my professors approached me with an opportunity to contribute to a project aimed at creating a comprehensive database application. Seeing the potential of this project, I decided to take on the challenge and develop a web-based database specifically for the rubber industry.
